Connect Four

Connect Four 🎯 is a 2-player game where you and a friend (or frenemy 👀) take turns dropping colored discs 🎨 into a grid. The goal? Be the first to line up 4 in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally - before your opponent does. Sounds simple, but trust... it gets intense real quick 😤🔥. One wrong move and it's game over 💀. Easy to play, hard to master - and yes, winning totally counts as a personality trait 🏆😎.
